## Source strategy for older history

- `Axios Des Moines` is the cleanest structured source for 2021-2022 openings and many closures.
- `Des Moines Register` often has stronger dining depth for closures and end-of-year recaps, but the site can be difficult to access directly.
- `Yahoo` and `AOL` mirrors of Register stories were used when they preserved the Register reporting and were easier to verify from this environment.
- `Cityview` was used selectively for retrospective confirmation on a few neighborhood mainstays.

## Verification choices

- `verified` means the event had a direct opening/closing report or a strong retrospective reference from a reputable outlet.
- `review` means the venue clearly existed in the timeline, but the exact opening/closing date still deserves one more primary confirmation.

## Remaining high-value gaps

- Des Moines Register year-end closure/opening roundups for 2021 and 2022 if we can capture fuller lists.
